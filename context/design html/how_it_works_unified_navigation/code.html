Interpreting the Score

Data science is a tool, not a crystal ball. Our model achieves an R² ≈ 0.39. This means while we can predict roughly 39% of a song's popularity through acoustic data alone, the rest remains in the realm of human culture.

The Missing 61%

Marketing budget, artist reputation, social media trends, and cultural timing are factors no algorithm can perfectly capture. Use these scores as a directional guide, not a definitive verdict.

Detailed FAQ

How accurate is the Success Probability score? expand_more
Our model achieves an 84% accuracy rate when predicting streaming growth within the first 3 months, based on the validation set from our 2023 dataset. However, "black swan" viral events cannot be predicted.
What genres are best supported by the AI? expand_more
The algorithm is currently optimized for Pop, Urban (Reggaeton/Trap), EDM, and Indie Rock. Classical and Jazz models are currently in beta under our specialized academic branch.
Why is my track flagged for "Low Entropy"? expand_more
Low entropy suggests that the musical progression is highly predictable. While this is common in some pop sub-genres, our model suggests it may lead to lower long-term listener retention in competitive playlists.
